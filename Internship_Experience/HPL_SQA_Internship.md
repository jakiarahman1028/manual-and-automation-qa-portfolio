# HPL SQA Internship Experience

**Role:** Software Quality Assurance (SQA) Intern  
**Organization:** Healthcare Pharmaceuticals Ltd.  
**Duration:** July 2025 – Present  

This document provides a detailed overview of my QA internship experience, demonstrating the scope of work, responsibilities, processes followed, and cross-team collaboration. All work described is presented in a professional, high-level manner.

---

## 1. Mobile Application Testing (HPL Android App)

**Scope:** Functional and usability testing of HPL’s Android enterprise application for internal and field operations.

**Responsibilities & Work Performed:**

- **Test Planning & Documentation:**  
  - Created **test plans** covering functional, smoke, sanity, regression, and usability testing.  
  - Designed **70+ detailed manual test cases** including preconditions, expected results, and steps.  
  - Developed **test scenarios** mapping to business requirements and user stories.  

- **Testing Execution:**  
  - Executed functional and regression test cases across multiple Android devices.  
  - Performed usability and UI/UX validation to ensure smooth user experience and adherence to design flows.  
  - Validated workflows for field operations, including data entry, reporting, and dashboard navigation.  

- **Defect Management:**  
  - Logged defects in **Jira** with clear reproduction steps, screenshots, and severity levels.  
  - Retested and verified fixes in collaboration with developers.  
  - Tracked issue resolution progress to ensure timely deployment.  

- **Collaboration:**  
  - Coordinated with **Android development team**, field representatives (Zonal Heads, MIO/SMIO, AM/RM), and QA lead for bug validation.  
  - Participated in sprint planning, daily stand-ups, and requirement clarification sessions.
